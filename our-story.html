Why ProKit Studio exists

Our Story

ProKit Studio started with a simple frustration: ordering custom gear shouldn’t feel like a project management job. Endless email chains, mockup delays, version confusion, and “close enough” approvals are the norm — even when everyone is trying to move fast. We built ProKit to put the configurator directly in the buyer’s hands.

The problem we saw

Custom gear orders have lots of small decisions (style, colors, patch type, placement, quantities). In most workflows, each change requires a supplier rep to recreate and resend a mockup. That slows the process, introduces mistakes, and makes approvals painful.

What we’re building

A simple, real-time configuration experience: pick a product, customize it visually, and submit a clean spec for production. No waiting. No back-and-forth. And approvals happen by link, not by email thread.

Who it’s for

Gear suppliers and the customers they serve: golf courses, sports teams, corporate buyers, events, and clubs. If you order branded gear and care about speed + accuracy, this is built for you.
